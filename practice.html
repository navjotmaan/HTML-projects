Indian Masala Tea

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making Masala Tea also known as Masala Chai! It is the most popular drink in India. A hot cup of tea is perfect to soothe your senses and refresh your mind. Follow this recipe to make sweet, spicy and hot Tea/Chai.

Ingredients

  • Black Tea Leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Spices like ginger,cardamom, cloves

Instructions

  1. Crush the spices properly
  2. Heat 11/2 cup of water
  3. Add spices and sugar to water
  4. Boil this for 2 -3 minutes
  5. Add milk to it and heat until it boil
